Opinion!

Wargaming lacks accessibility and it is destroying both the playebase and developers. Wargaming is a niche hobby but there are some larger wargmames with a dedicated playerbase. Wargaming is constanly evolving. Developers are making their gameplay more complex, dynamic, and challenging but they aren't improving tutorials or onboarding. The developers boast about lenghty dry manuals. They brag about complex mechanics. This causes new and even seasoned wargamers to avoid the games. We as wargamers dedicate more time to learning a game than acutally playing it. We buy 500 page manuals and highlight critical information. By the time you have the knowledge to play the game a new iteration or wargame has probably released. This is a viscious cycle! It is time we as a community solve this problem.
